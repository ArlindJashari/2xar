About Us

Engineering The
Future Landmarks

2XAR represents the intersection of technical precision and visionary engineering. We focus on four core sectors: Buildings, Infrastructure, Water, and Roads. We move beyond conventional construction to develop complex infrastructure that powers regional economies and defines the architectural standards of the Western Balkans.

Based in Prishtina, our 25-year legacy is built on a foundation of absolute reliability. We integrate advanced civil engineering with strategic project management to deliver high-impact works that stand the test of time and support regional growth.
