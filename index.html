About Us

Engineering The
Future Landmarks

2XAR represents the intersection of technical precision and visionary engineering. We operate across four core sectors: Buildings, Infrastructure, Water, and Roads - delivering complex projects that power regional economies and shape the built environment of Southeast Europe.

With headquarters in Prishtina, and offices in Tirana, Podgorica, and Skopje, 2XAR brings together a team with extensive experience developed over three decades across multiple sectors and geographies, including Southeast and Western Europe. This foundation allows us to combine advanced engineering with disciplined project execution, delivering infrastructure defined by reliability, performance, and long-term value.
