Our Legacy

For over two decades, we have been shaping the skyline and infrastructure of Southeast Europe. From humble beginnings to regional leadership.

1999

Laying the First Stone

In the aftermath of conflict, 2XAR was founded in Prishtina with a singular mission: to rebuild. We started with essential residential reconstruction, prioritizing speed without compromising stability.

2005

Major Transit & Bridges

Our first breakthrough in civil infrastructure. We successfully delivered the R-7 Connector Bridge, proving our capacity to handle complex engineering challenges and large-scale logistics.

2015

Regional Expansion

Opening offices in Albania and Macedonia, 2XAR transitioned from a local builder to a regional powerhouse, undertaking cross-border energy and water treatment projects.

2024

The Industry Standard

Today, with over €1.5B in completed projects, we stand as the benchmark for quality. We are now pioneering green construction and smart city integrations across the Western Balkans.
